QuickCGPA - Delete Account & Data

We respect your privacy. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time.

How to Delete Your Account

1. Open the QuickCGPA app
2. Go to Settings (gear icon in top right)
3. Scroll down to Account section
4. Tap "Delete Account"
5. Confirm deletion in the popup dialog

What Gets Deleted

  • Your login credentials
  • All semester results and GPA data
  • All subjects and grades
  • Your account from our system

Data Retention

All data is permanently deleted immediately. This action cannot be undone.
